Frequently asked.

No. Commercial AI image generation is allowed. We follow App Store Guidelines 4.7 + privacy manifest requirements. Generated content is yours.

Can I use these images for ads on TikTok / Meta?

Yes. All generated content is licensed for your commercial use across any platform.

Do you store my photos?

For 90 days on Free tier, 12 months on paid. You can delete them anytime. We never use your photos to train AI.

What marketplaces are supported at launch?

Shopify (direct upload), Amazon (download formatted), TikTok Shop (download formatted), Noon (download formatted), Meta Ads (formatted), Google Shopping (formatted). More coming.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel via iOS Settings → Subscriptions. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.

What about Arabic / Spanish / multi-language?

Arabic + Spanish launching in v1.1. Listing translation features coming.

How is this different from Photoroom Ultra?

Photoroom is excellent for Shopify-only sellers — they added AI video + Shopify direct upload at Ultra tier. HeroShot's wedge: multi-marketplace. Plus AI video is in our $99 Pro tier, not $$$ Ultra. If you sell on 2+ marketplaces, HeroShot pays for itself in time saved cropping + re-uploading.
